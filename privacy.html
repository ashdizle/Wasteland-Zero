Privacy Policy for Wasteland Zero

Last updated: April 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Wasteland Zero ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in our mobile game application for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

Information We Collect

Wasteland Zero is designed with privacy in mind. We minimize data collection and respect your privacy:

  • Local Game Data: All game progress, saves, and settings are stored locally on your device
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or other personal identifiers
  • No Account Required: You can play the full game without creating an account

Local Data Storage

The following data is stored on your device only:

  • Game saves and progress (character stats, inventory, achievements)
  • Game settings (audio preferences, difficulty)
  • Unlocked content (cosmetics, achievements, prestige bonuses)

Important: This data is not transmitted to our servers and remains on your device. If you delete the app, this data will be removed.

Third-Party Services

Wasteland Zero may use the following third-party services provided by Apple:

  • In-App Purchases: Processed by Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies.
  • Game Center (Optional): If you choose to use Game Center for achievements and leaderboards, Apple's privacy policy applies.

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

In-App Purchases

Wasteland Zero offers optional cosmetic in-app purchases (character skins, dice skins, UI themes) and convenience items (XP boosts). All purchases are:

  • Processed securely through Apple's App Store
  • Subject to Apple's privacy policy and terms
  • Optional and do not affect core gameplay

We do not receive or store your payment information.

Children's Privacy

Wasteland Zero is rated 13+ and is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Data Security

Since all game data is stored locally on your device, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Using a device passcode or biometric lock
  • Keeping your iOS software up to date
  • Using iCloud backup to preserve your game progress (optional)

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your game data is accessible within the app
  • Deletion: Delete the app to remove all locally stored data
  • Portability: Game data is stored in standard formats on your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Posting a notice in the app (for significant changes)

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

International Users

Wasteland Zero is available worldwide. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data is transferred internationally by us.

No Sale of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Since we don't collect personal information, there is nothing to sell.
