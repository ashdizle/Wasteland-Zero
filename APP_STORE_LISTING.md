## Description (4000 characters max)

Survive the wasteland. Master the dice. Become a legend.

Welcome to WASTELAND ZERO, where humanity's last survivors fight for existence in a radioactive world. This isn't just another RPG—it's a dice-rolling, turn-based roguelite where every decision matters and death is permanent.

**FORGE YOUR LEGEND**

Choose from 5 unique races and 10 distinct character archetypes, each with their own playstyle. Are you a tactical Sniper picking off enemies from afar? A brutal Berserker charging into battle? Or a cunning Engineer deploying shock weapons? With dozens of traits and skills to unlock, no two runs are ever the same.

**STRATEGIC D20 COMBAT**

Combat isn't about button-mashing—it's about managing AP, timing your attacks, and knowing when to BRACE for that life-saving heal. Roll the D20 for devastating power attacks, activate VATS for precision shots, or flee when overwhelmed. Your dice rolls determine your fate.

**EXPLORE A DANGEROUS WORLD**

Navigate a procedurally-generated wasteland filled with Raiders, Ghouls, Mutants, and terrifying bosses. Each tile could hide loot, enemies, or deadly radiation. Choose your path wisely—retreat is always an option, but fortune favors the bold.

**DEEP PROGRESSION SYSTEMS**

- **Prestige & Rebirth**: Ascend beyond Level 100 to unlock permanent bonuses
- **Crafting System**: Forge legendary weapons from salvaged materials
- **Equipment Tiers**: From Common junk to Celestial artifacts
- **Achievements**: 26 challenges to conquer (Bronze, Silver, Gold)
- **Bounty System**: Hunt specific enemies for caps and rewards

**ROGUELITE DONE RIGHT**

Permanent death means every run matters, but you're not starting from zero. Unlock new classes, earn prestige bonuses, and discover game-changing items that persist across playthroughs. Master the mechanics, learn enemy patterns, and push further each time.

**FEATURES**

✓ 10 Character Archetypes (Warrior, Scavenger, Doc, Grunt, Sniper, Pyro, Gunslinger, Berserker, Ghost, Engineer)
✓ Turn-based D20 combat with strategic depth
✓ Procedural wasteland generation—every run is unique
✓ 100+ items including weapons, armor, consumables, and artifacts
✓ Epic boss battles against legendary foes
✓ Prestige system for endless progression
✓ Cosmetic shop—customize your survivor's appearance
✓ Dark humor and post-apocalyptic storytelling
✓ Portrait + Landscape support
✓ Offline playable—no internet required

**A LOVE LETTER TO CLASSIC RPGs**

Wasteland Zero combines the strategic depth of classic roguelikes with modern design. Inspired by Fallout, Darkest Dungeon, and FTL, this is a game for players who love tactical combat, meaningful choices, and the thrill of beating impossible odds.

**NO PAY-TO-WIN**

All purchases are cosmetic only (character skins, dice skins, UI themes). Skill and strategy determine your success, not your wallet. The wasteland doesn't care how much you've spent—it only cares if you can survive.

**JOIN THE SURVIVORS**

The wasteland awaits. Will you become a legend, or just another radiation-bleached skeleton?

Download now and roll the dice on your survival.
