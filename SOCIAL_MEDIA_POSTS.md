- **Mobile optimization** - Getting touch controls to feel good took forever

## About

Wasteland Zero is a post-apocalyptic roguelite RPG where every decision matters and every fight is decided by the roll of a D20. Built in the spirit of classic tabletop RPGs and modern roguelikes, it combines strategic turn-based combat with procedural generation and permanent consequences.

🎲 **D20 Combat System**
Roll for attacks, dodge incoming strikes, and pray for critical hits. Your stats improve your chances, but fate still plays a role.

Mysterious tears in spacetime appear randomly. Step through for powerful rewards... or devastating consequences.

When you die, you're dead. But your achievements unlock new classes, items, and starting bonuses for future runs.

👾 **Boss Fights**

🏆 **Global Leaderboards**
